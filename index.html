üê∑ Pigs are Super Smart!

Pigs are one of the smartest animals in the world - even smarter than many dogs! They can learn tricks, play video games (yes, really!), recognize their own names, and even understand complex emotions.

Pigs love to roll in mud to keep cool and protect their skin from the sun. They're actually very clean animals and will never mess in the place where they sleep if given enough space!

Pigs can dream while they sleep, just like you! They also have excellent memories and can remember things for years.

üêÑ Cows Have Best Friends

Cows are incredibly social animals who form deep friendships! They choose best friends and become stressed when separated. Mother cows have strong bonds with their babies and will call to them when they're apart.

Cows are gentle, curious animals who love to explore. They can recognize over 100 other cows and remember faces for a long time - even human faces!

Cows produce more milk when they listen to slow, calming music! Some farmers even play classical music for their cows. üéµ

üêî Chickens Talk to Their Babies

Mother hens start talking to their chicks before they even hatch! The babies can hear their mom through the eggshell and will chirp back. Chickens have over 30 different vocalizations to communicate different things.

Chickens are smarter than people think - they can do math, recognize over 100 different chickens, and even plan for the future!

Chickens experience REM sleep, which means they dream just like we do! They also love to play and have fun races with each other.

üêë Sheep Have Amazing Memories

Sheep can remember and recognize up to 50 different sheep and 10 human faces for years! They're much smarter than people give them credit for. Sheep also have best friends and get stressed when separated from them.

Sheep show emotions through their facial expressions. Scientists have learned that sheep can feel happy, sad, scared, and even bored!

Sheep can self-medicate! When they feel sick, they'll seek out specific plants that can help them feel better. üåø

üåæ Understanding Factory Farms

Sometimes, many animals are kept in small spaces to produce food quickly and cheaply. This can make animals uncomfortable and sad because they don't have room to move around, play, or express their natural behaviors.

Good news! More and more farms are choosing to give animals better lives. Some farms let animals roam outside, play in the grass, and live in comfortable homes. These are called "free-range" or "pasture-raised" farms, and they're becoming more popular!

  • ü•ï Eat more plants! Fruits, vegetables, beans, and grains are delicious and help animals
  • üíö Choose kindly-raised products when you can (look for free-range or pasture-raised labels)
  • üì¢ Share what you learn with friends and family in a kind way
  • üé® Create and celebrate! Draw pictures, write poems, or make crafts about animals you love

üåû Hi there! I'm Penny!

Let me tell you about my perfect day! I wake up when the sun peeks through my cozy barn. The first thing I do? Roll in my favorite mud puddle! It's not just fun - mud is like sunscreen for pigs. It keeps me cool and protects my sensitive skin.

Then I meet up with my best friends, Pebbles and Petunia. We love solving puzzles together! Our farmer gives us treat-dispensing toys, and we figure out how to get the yummy snacks inside. I'm really good at it!

In the afternoon, I love getting belly rubs. Did you know I can recognize everyone by their voice? When my farmer calls my name, I come running! I even learned to sit on command - just like a dog!

üí≠ What I want you to know: Just like you, I have feelings. I get excited when I see my friends. I feel safest in my cozy bed at night. I love learning new things. Every pig deserves to be happy, play, and be loved!

üêÑ

Clara the Cow's Friendship

Click to read my story!

üåº Hello friend! I'm Clara!

I want to tell you about my best friend, Bella. We do everything together! We graze in the same spots, nap under the same tree, and even moo in harmony (it's a cow thing!).

When Bella had to go to the vet once, I was so worried! I kept calling for her and wouldn't eat much. When she came back, I was SO happy! We touched noses and I could finally relax. That's how much cows care about our friends.

We spend our days walking in the green fields, munching on fresh grass, and watching clouds. Sometimes we play tag (yes, cows play!). We also love our farmer because she's always gentle and talks to us in a soft voice.

üí≠ What I want you to know: Cows feel love and friendship deeply. When I had my calf, I sang to her every day. We remember kindness forever. All cows deserve space to roam, friends to love, and farmers who care!

Henry the Hen's Family

Click to read my story!

ü•ö Cluck cluck! I'm Henry!

The best day of my life was when my chicks hatched! I had been sitting on my eggs for weeks, singing to them every day. I could hear tiny chirps from inside the shells, and my babies chirped back to me!

When they finally peeked out, fluffy and yellow, my heart was so full! I taught them everything - how to scratch for bugs, where the best dust baths are, and which sounds mean "danger" versus "yummy food found!"

I love starting my day by scratching in the dirt hunting for tasty bugs. Then I take a dust bath to clean my feathers. In the evening, I roost with my family and friends, and we all nestle together.

üí≠ What I want you to know: Chickens are smart! I can recognize over 100 other chickens and understand cause and effect. I love my family and feel proud of being a mom. All chickens deserve to spread their wings, feel sunshine, and care for their babies!

I have the best memory in my flock! I can remember my shepherd's face and every sheep I've ever met. My best friend, Snowflake, and I have been together since we were lambs. We always stay close and share the best grazing spots.

One day, I wasn't feeling well, so I went looking for some herbs I knew would help me. My shepherd was amazed! Sheep can self-medicate - we know which plants make us feel better.

üíú Why Did We Create This Website?

We believe that learning about animals and where our food comes from is important - but it should never be scary, overwhelming, or traumatic. Many websites and videos show upsetting content that can hurt young learners emotionally.

That's why we created a different kind of space. Trigger-Free Facts was made to teach with kindness, honesty, and compassion. We want you to learn the truth about farm animals in a way that feels safe, gentle, and empowering.

üåü What Makes Us Different?

‚ú® No Shocking or Scary Content: We use gentle words, kind illustrations, and positive framing instead of upsetting images or harsh language.

üíö Emotional Support Built-In: We include tools like breathing exercises, affirmations, and mood check-ins to help you process what you learn.

üìö Age-Appropriate Education: Everything here is designed specifically for kids aged 5-12, with careful, caring language and concepts.

üéØ Action-Focused: We don't just talk about problems - we focus on what YOU can do to make a positive difference!

üèÜ Interactive Learning: Quizzes, stories, and activities make learning fun and memorable.

This website was created by a student who cares deeply about animals AND about making sure young people have access to honest, kind, trauma-free education. We believe kids like you are compassionate, intelligent, and capable of changing the world! üåç

üìú Our Promise to You

We promise to always be honest, but never scary. We'll teach you important facts, but always with deep care for your feelings and emotional safety. We'll remind you that learning about tough topics shows you have a big, brave, caring heart. And we'll always celebrate the positive changes happening in the world! üíñ

üåà Who Is This Website For?

This site is perfect for curious kids aged 5-12 who want to learn about animals. It's also a great resource for parents and teachers looking for age-appropriate, trauma-sensitive animal welfare education. If you care about animals and want to learn in a safe space, you're in the right place! üè†
